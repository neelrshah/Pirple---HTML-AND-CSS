Pav Bhaji

Ingredients :

  • Mixed Vegetables( potato, cauliflower, carrot, beans, capsicum)
  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Tomatoes
  • Peas
  • Water
  • Green Chillies
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Coriander Powder
  • Cumin Powder
  • Garam Masala Powder
  • Amchur (Dry Mango Powder)
  • Lime Juice
  • Coriander Leaves
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Boil the vegetables in a pressure cooker with 1 cup of water, for one whistle. Keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add chopped onions and fry till they turn golden brown.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and fry for 5 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes, green chillies, turmeric powder, chilli powder, coriander powder, cumin powder,garam masala powder and fry for a minute till all the masalas are well combined.
  5. Add the vegetables with the water, salt and mix well. Mash the vegetables well with a spoon.
  6. Add the amchur powder and mix well. Add the rest of the water to get a thick gravy.
  7. Remove from fire, add lime juice and mix. Garnish with the coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with pav.
