Pav Bhaji

Pav bhaji is a fast food dish from India. It consists of a thick vegetable curry served with a soft bread roll.Its origins are in the state of Maharashtra. I like this dish because of the taste and butter present in it.

Ingredients :

  • Mixed Vegetables( potato, cauliflower, carrot, beans, capsicum)
  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Tomatoes
  • Peas
  • Water
  • Green Chillies
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Coriander Powder
  • Cumin Powder
  • Garam Masala Powder
  • Amchur (Dry Mango Powder)
  • Lime Juice
  • Coriander Leaves
  • Salt
